ABOUT

The Chester County BBQ Festival in Henderson, Tennessee, offers mouthwatering BBQ, live music, quirky contests like Hog Callin', and a family-friendly atmosphere. Enjoy a fun, free day supporting local vendors on historic Main Street.

111 East Main Street
PO Box 1976
Henderson, TN 38340

FAQ

Are there accommodations for people with disabilities?

Yes, there are ramps on almost every sidewalk at the event.

Are animals welcome?

Absolutely! As long as they are on a leash!

Are there gluten free options?

Yes, certain vendors will have gluten free options.
